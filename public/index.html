WHO?

Amino Belyamani

Amino is a composer and multi-instrumentalist. Since the pandemic prompted the music industry to a standstill, he turned to another passion: web development. As sole creator and developer, he hand coded this entire site from scratch, for a smoother user experience.

aminobelyamani.com

WHY?

Perspective

There is no shortage of COVID-19 data; however, it is often difficult to decode. In contrast to a cold and stale string of numbers, this interactive map presents a visual context for the current state of the global pandemic.
